Frequently Asked Questions

Who can vote?

Generally, any citizen who is 18 years of age or older on the qualifying date can vote, provided they are registered in the electoral roll and not disqualified by law.

What documents are required to vote?

You typically need a valid Voter ID card or other approved government-issued photo identification (like a Passport, Driving License, or National ID).

What is an EVM?

An Electronic Voting Machine (EVM) is a device used to record votes electronically, ensuring a faster, more accurate, and paperless voting process.
